Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Jayanti 2025

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti is celebrated to honor the birth anniversary of the great Maratha warrior, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who laid the foundation of the Maratha Empire.

About Event

Jayanti 2025

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti features traditional dances, music, and storytelling that celebrate his legacy. Renowned historians and leaders deliver speeches on his contributions to Indian history. Exhibitions showcase artifacts, weapons, and maps of the Maratha Empire. The event also includes painting and essay competitions inspired by Shivaji Maharaj’s life and ideals.
